## Registration

Visit [getrolio.app/register](https://getrolio.app/register) to create your account.

You'll need:
- A valid email address
- A password (minimum 8 characters)
- Your organization's name

## Organization Setup

After registering, complete your organization profile:

**Organization Name** — The name displayed on your directory (e.g., "Hillside Community Church")

**Organization Type** — Helps Rolio customize your experience:
- Church
- Nonprofit
- Club or Association
- Law Firm
- Alumni Group
- Professional Association
- Other

**Subdomain** — Your directory URL: `getrolio.app/view/[subdomain]`. Auto-generated from your org name but can be customized.

## Admin Account

Your registration email becomes the primary admin account. You can invite additional admins later from **Admin → Settings → Team**.

<Warning>
Admin seat limits apply based on your plan: 1 admin on Starter, 3 on Pro, unlimited on Organization.
</Warning>
