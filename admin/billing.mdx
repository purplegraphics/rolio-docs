## Current Plan

View your current plan at **Billing** in the sidebar.

The billing page shows:
- Current plan and status
- Next billing date
- Member usage vs. plan limit
- Options to upgrade or manage your subscription

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Select the plan you want to upgrade to
3. Choose monthly or annual billing (save 20% annually)
4. Complete checkout via Stripe

Your new plan activates immediately after payment.

## Managing Your Subscription

Click **Manage subscription** to open the Stripe Customer Portal where you can:
- Update payment method
- Download invoices
- Cancel subscription

## Cancellation

When you cancel:
- You retain access until the end of your current billing period
- Your data is preserved for 90 days after cancellation
- Export your data anytime before deletion
