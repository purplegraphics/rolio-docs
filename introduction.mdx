## Why Rolio?

Every other directory tool was built before AI existed. Rolio is built AI-first:

- **Magic link self-update** — members update their own profiles in 30 seconds, no login required
- **Semantic search** — find members with natural language queries
- **Automated board reports** — generate membership summaries with one prompt
- **Private by default** — granular field-level privacy controls
- **Flat-rate pricing** — never penalized for growing
