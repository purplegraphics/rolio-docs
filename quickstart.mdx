---
title: "Quick Start"
description: "Get your Rolio directory up and running in under 5 minutes."
---

## Step 1: Create your account

Go to [getrolio.app/register](https://getrolio.app/register) and sign up with your email address.

<Note>
No credit card required. All plans include a 30-day free trial.
</Note>

## Step 2: Set up your organization

After registering, you'll be prompted to create your organization:

1. Enter your **organization name** (e.g., "St. Matthew's Church")
2. Select your **organization type** (church, nonprofit, club, etc.)
3. Your directory URL is automatically generated: `getrolio.app/view/your-org-name`

## Step 3: Add your first members

You can add members in three ways:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Add manually">
    Click **+ Add member** in the directory and fill in the member's details.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import CSV">
    Go to **Admin → Members → Import CSV** and upload a spreadsheet.
    Rolio's AI will map your columns automatically.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Send magic links">
    Once you have members, send them a magic link to fill in their own details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 4: Share your directory

Your public directory is available at `getrolio.app/view/your-slug`.

Go to **Admin → Overview** to:
- Copy your directory link
- Generate a QR code
- Configure what information is publicly visible

## Step 5: Send magic links

The fastest way to keep your directory accurate is to send members a magic link:

1. Go to **Admin → Magic Links**
2. Click **Send Update Requests**
3. Select **All members** or a specific group
4. Members receive an email with a personal link to review and update their profile

<Tip>
Members can update their profile in under 30 seconds — no login or password required.
</Tip>
