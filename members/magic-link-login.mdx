## What is Magic Link Login?

Instead of creating yet another account and password, Rolio lets you verify your identity with a one-time link sent to your email. This link logs you into the member directory for 30 days.

## How to Log In

1. Visit your organization's directory: `getrolio.app/view/[org-slug]`
2. Click **Member? Sign in**
3. Enter your email address
4. Click **Send Access Link**
5. Check your email for a message from Rolio
6. Click **Access Member Directory** in the email
7. You're in!

<Note>
Your email must already be in the directory for this to work. If you don't receive an email, contact your organization admin to verify your email address is on file.
</Note>

## Session Duration

Your session lasts **30 days** from the time you clicked the link. After 30 days, you'll need to request a new link.

## Security

- Each link can only be used once
- Links expire after 24 hours if unused
- Your session is stored securely in your browser
- We never send your link to anyone else
