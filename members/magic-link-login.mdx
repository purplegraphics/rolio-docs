## How to Log In

1. Visit your organization's directory: `getrolio.app/view/[org-slug]`
2. Click **Member? Sign in**
3. Enter your email address
4. Click **Send Access Link**
5. Check your email for a message from Rolio
6. Click **Access Member Directory**
7. You're in!

<Note>
  Your email must already be in the directory. If you don't receive an email, contact your organization admin.
</Note>

## Session Duration

Your session lasts **30 days** from when you clicked the link. After 30 days, request a new link.

## Security

- Each link can only be used once
- Links expire after 24 hours if unused
- Your session is stored securely in your browser
- We never send your link to anyone else
