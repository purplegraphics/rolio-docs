title: "Updating Your Profile"
description: "How to update your information in the directory."
---

## Via Magic Link

1. You receive an email: **"\[Org Name\] — Please update your directory info"**
2. Click **Review & Update My Info**
3. Review your current information
4. Make changes (name, phone, address, photo, bio, social links)
5. Click **Submit My Update**

Your update goes to your admin for review unless auto-approved.

<Note>
  Magic links are personal to you and expire in 7 days. Do not forward them.
</Note>

## From Your Profile Page

1. Visit the directory and find your profile
2. Click **Update my info**
3. A magic link is sent to your email
4. Click the link to open your update form

## What You Can Update

- First and last name
- Phone number
- Address (street, city, state, zip)
- Profile photo and cover photo
- Bio (max 500 characters)
- Birthday (month and day, year optional)
- Social links (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, website)

## Photo Tips

- **Profile photo:** Minimum 200×200px, square works best
- **Cover photo:** Minimum 1400×400px, landscape orientation
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP (max 5MB)
