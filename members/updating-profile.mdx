## Via Magic Link

The most common way to update your profile:

1. You receive an email from your organization with the subject line **"[Org Name] — Please update your directory info"**
2. Click **Review & Update My Info**
3. Review your current information
4. Make any changes (name, phone, address, photo, bio, social links)
5. Click **Submit My Update**

Your update goes to your organization admin for review (unless auto-approved).

<Note>
Magic links are personal to you and expire in 7 days. Do not forward them.
</Note>

## From Your Profile Page

If you are logged in as a member:

1. Visit the directory and find your profile
2. Click **Update my info**
3. A magic link is sent to your email
4. Click the link to open your update form

## What You Can Update

- First and last name
- Phone number
- Address (street, city, state, zip)
- Profile photo
- Cover photo
- Bio (about text, max 500 characters)
- Birthday (month and day, year optional)
- Social links (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, website)

## Photo Tips

- **Profile photo:** Square or portrait orientation works best. Minimum 200×200px.
- **Cover photo:** Landscape orientation. Minimum 1400×400px.
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP (max 5MB)
