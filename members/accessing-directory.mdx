## Public Access

Your organization's public directory is available at: `https://www.getrolio.app/view/[org-slug]`

Ask your organization admin for the link.

## Member Login

To access additional member information:

1. Visit the directory link
2. Click **Member? Sign in** (top right)
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a one-time access link
5. Click the link to access the full member view

<Note>
  Your email must already be in the directory. If you don't receive an email, contact your admin to verify your email is on file.
</Note>

## Searching the Directory

Use the search bar to find members by name, email, phone number, or group.

## Viewing a Member Profile

Click any member card to view their full profile, including all fields your admin has made visible.
