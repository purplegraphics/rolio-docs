## Public Access

Your organization's public directory is available at:
`https://www.getrolio.app/view/[org-slug]`

Ask your organization admin for the link, or look for it in any email from Rolio.

Depending on your organization's settings, you may see:
- Member names and photos
- Group memberships
- Bio and social links
- Contact information (if admin has enabled it)

## Member Login

To access additional member information, verify your email:

1. Visit the directory link
2. Click **Member? Sign in** (top right)
3. Enter your email address — must match what's in the directory
4. Check your email for a one-time access link
5. Click the link to access the full member view

<Note>
Access links expire after 24 hours. Request a new one if needed.
</Note>

## Searching the Directory

Use the search bar to find members by:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Group

## Viewing a Member Profile

Click any member card to view their full profile, including all fields your admin has made visible.
