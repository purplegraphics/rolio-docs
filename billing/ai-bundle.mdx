## What is the AI Power Bundle?

The AI Power Bundle is a **\$29/month add-on** that unlocks advanced AI capabilities on any Rolio plan.

## Included Features

### AI Admin Chatbot

Ask your directory questions in plain English:

- _"How many members do we have in each group?"_
- _"Send magic links to everyone in the Youth Ministry"_
- _"Who is missing a phone number?"_

### Semantic Search

Find members with natural language:

- _"Members who work in healthcare"_
- _"Everyone in the worship team missing a photo"_

### AI Directory Health Score

A 0-100 score showing how complete and accurate your directory is, with action items to improve it.

### Automated Board Reports

Type what you need, get a formatted PDF:

- _"Generate a membership summary for Tuesday's board meeting"_

### Smart Member Matching

Surfaces connections between members based on skills, interests, and roles.

### Renewal Risk Scoring

Identifies members at risk of disengaging before they leave.

## Usage Limits

| Feature | Monthly Limit |
| --- | --- |
| AI Chatbot queries | 500 |
| Semantic searches | 1,000 |
| Board reports | 10 |

Usage resets on your billing anniversary date.

## Adding the Bundle

1. Go to **Billing** in your dashboard
2. Check **AI Power Bundle — Add to checkout**
3. Complete checkout

The bundle activates immediately.
