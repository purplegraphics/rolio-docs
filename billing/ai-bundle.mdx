## What is the AI Power Bundle?

The AI Power Bundle is a $29/month add-on that unlocks advanced AI capabilities on any Rolio plan.

## Included Features

### AI Admin Chatbot
Ask your directory questions in plain English and take bulk actions:
- *"How many members do we have in each group?"*
- *"Send magic links to everyone in the Youth Ministry"*
- *"Who is missing a phone number?"*
- *"Draft a welcome email to members who joined this month"*

### Semantic Search
Find members with natural language queries:
- *"Members who work in healthcare"*
- *"Everyone in the worship team missing a photo"*
- *"Members who haven't updated in over a year"*

### AI Directory Health Score
A 0-100 score showing how complete and accurate your directory is, with specific action items to improve it.

### Automated Board Reports
Type what you need, get a formatted PDF:
- *"Generate a membership summary for Tuesday's board meeting"*

### Smart Member Matching
Surfaces connections between members based on skills, interests, and roles.

### Renewal Risk Scoring
Identifies members at risk of disengaging before they leave.

## Usage Limits

| Feature | Monthly Limit |
|---|---|
| AI Chatbot queries | 500 |
| Semantic searches | 1,000 |
| Board reports generated | 10 |

Usage resets on your billing anniversary date.

## Adding the Bundle

1. Go to **Billing** in your dashboard
2. Check **AI Power Bundle — Add to checkout**
3. Complete checkout

The bundle activates immediately and works alongside your existing plan.
